Brief Biography


A hearty Welcome! 

I am an Faculty at Dept. of Computer Science Engineering, Manipal Academy of Higher Education (MAHE), India.

Prior to coming to MAHE, I spent five wonderful years as full-time research scholar at Siddaganga Institute Of Technology, India (hosted by Prof. N R Sunitha).

Born in a remote village of Karnataka, I graduated from Sri Krishnarajendra Silver Jubilee Technological Institute, Bengaluru, India and completed my master's degree in Computer Networks and Engineering (CNE) from Siddaganga Institute Of Technology, India.

Apart from science, I have inclinations towards poet writing. I enjoy playing various sports such as cricket, shuttlecock and yoga.

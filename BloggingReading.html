Reading


I love reading various types of books. As a kid, I got exposed to rich bengali literature. I grew up relishing books by legendary poets and writers like Rabidranath Tagore, Satyajit Ray, Bibhutibhushan Bandyopadhyay, Sukumar Ray, Bankim Chandra Chattopadhyay, Sarat Chandra Chattopadhyay, Sharadindu Bandyopadhyay, Sunil Gangopadhyay, Dwijendralal Ray, Ashapoorna Devi, Upendrakishore Ray Chowdhury, Kazi Nazrul Islam to name a few. In my teens, I loved the bengali translations of the creations by Arthur Conan Doyle , Jules Verne, William Shakespeare , Herge. I try to keep my reading habit consistent in spite of the changing phases of my life. Below is the list of the books I have gone through recently and enjoyed thoroughly. Try these books, you will not regret, I promise! I will keep adding my new additions.

Teaching

Teaching is the essence of an academic institute. Both the involved parties- the teacher and the students- are enriched and enlightened in the process of teaching. My opinion about a good teacher is reflected by the following quotes: “What is a teacher? I'll tell you: it isn't someone who teaches something, but someone who inspires the student to give of her best in order to discover what she already knows.” | “The best teacher is not the one who knows most but the one who is most capable of reducing knowledge to that simple compound of the obvious and wonderful.” | ``It is the supreme art of the teacher to awaken joy in creative expression and knowledge."

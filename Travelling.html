Travelling


I am an ardent travel freak. Travelling rejuvenates me like nothing. It's my most favorite passtime. Being in love with nature and history, places with natural exuberance and historic importance are my top favorites. In a visit to a place with natural bunty, I soak in every moment I spent with the nature and absorb plethora of positive energy. In a visit to a place with historical touch, I throughly learn the minute details of the past relevant to the place and then feel and live with the past during my up, close and personal moments with the place. Therefore every outing elevates my consciousness, enriches my knowledge, readies my mind for more openness and off course, transforms me to a better being.
